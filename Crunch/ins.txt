crunch version 3.6

Crunch can create a wordlist based on criteria you specify.  The output from crunch can be sent to file.

Usage: crunch <min> <max> [options]
where min and max are numbers

Ex: crunch 2 3 -d num

Please refer to the man page for instructions and examples on how to use crunch.
To get to the MAN page  type "crunch --help".
